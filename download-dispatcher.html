Dispatch Again

Review what actually happened after each download, including final saved path, filename, workspace, route, and Dispatch Again activity.

Profiles

Export and import profiles to back up your setup or move Download Dispatcher settings to another browser profile.

Important Dispatch Again note

Dispatch Again does not delete, move, or clean up the earlier file. It starts a new repeat download through the browser and dispatches that repeat download to the current resolved destination. Some generated, expired, local, or site-protected downloads may not be repeatable by the browser.
